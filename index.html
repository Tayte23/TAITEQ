FAQ

Do I need to be technical to use TAITEQ?

No. It’s built for non-technical founders. Connect your tools, pick your agents, and you’re ready.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We use encryption in transit and at rest. You control connected tools and can disconnect anytime.

What does “tasks/month” mean?

Each discrete action an agent performs (e.g., drafting an email, summarising a page) counts as a task. Limits are generous.

Can I start on mobile?

Absolutely. TAITEQ is designed mobile-first so your team lives in your pocket.
